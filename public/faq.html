FAQ

ThriftEx is an online marketplace for buying and selling pre-loved clothing. We connect buyers and sellers in a secure and easy-to-use platform that supports negotiations, payments, shipping, and order tracking.

It’s designed for fashion lovers who want great deals and eco-conscious sellers who want an easy-to-manage dashboard.

We accept secure online payments through Razorpay, which supports credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and major digital wallets.

You can request a cancellation before the seller ships the product. Once shipped, our return/refund policy will apply.

We are currently not accepting any returns or providing refunds. We are working towards including that feature soon!

You will be charged immediately after checkout, once the payment is successfully processed.
