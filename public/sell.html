Create a Great Listing

Here are six ways to set yourself up for success.

Write a Clear & Catchy Title

  • Use specific details like brand, size, and condition to make your listing easy to find
  • Avoid all caps and keep it concise yet informative.

Upload High-Quality Photos

  • Take well-lit photos from multiple angles to showcase your item clearly.
  • Highlight any unique details or imperfections for transparency.

Write a Detailed Description

  • Provide essential information like fabric, fit, and any wear-and-tear details.
  • A well-written description builds trust with buyers.

Set a Fair Price & Be Open to Offers

  • Research similar listings to price competitively, but stay open to negotiations.
  • Buyers can send offers, and you have the flexibility to accept, decline, or counter to get the best deal.

Make Your Listing Stand Out

  • Use accurate keywords and detailed descriptions to make your listing more searchable and appealing.
  • The more information you provide, the faster it sells!

Ship Smoothly & Responsibly

  • Once a deal is finalized, pack your item securely and our delivery partner will come pick it up.
  • Quick and reliable shipping improves buyer satisfaction and increases your chances of repeat sales.
