Who We Are

At ThriftEx, we’re more than a marketplace—we’re a community dedicated to sustainability, affordability, and style. Our mission is to make fashion accessible and eco-friendly by giving pre-loved clothing a second chance. Every purchase supports a greener future, promoting reuse over waste while connecting buyers and sellers in a space where quality fashion gets a new life.


Why Choose ThriftEx?

At ThriftEx, we make sustainable fashion easy, affordable, and rewarding. Here’s why we stand out:

  • Sustainable & Stylish – Shop pre-loved fashion that’s trendy, high-quality, and eco-friendly.
  • Affordable Finds – Get your hands on unique pieces at prices that won’t break the bank.
  • Seamless Buying & Selling – A user-friendly platform that makes thrifting effortless for both buyers and sellers.
  • Smart Shopping, Zero Waste – Every purchase helps reduce fashion waste and supports a more sustainable future.


People

ThriftEx is the vision of Mohini and Insiya, who co-founded and developed the platform with a shared passion for sustainable fashion. As a dedicated two-person team we are passionate about making second-hand shopping seamless, stylish, and accessible for all!
